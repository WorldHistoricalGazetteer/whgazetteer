Making a WHG collection public

By definition, a new collection can only contain datasets already designated "public." However, new collections are initially "private"—that is, visible only to you (the "owner") and to the WHG team. Before a collection goes public, the WHG editorial team will do a quick review to ensure the metadata is sufficient, and that the public screens displaying its contents are performing properly.

If you are ready to make your collection public, please let us know with this contact form.
