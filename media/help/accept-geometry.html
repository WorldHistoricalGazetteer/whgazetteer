Accept geometries in matches

In the Review step that follows initiating a reconciliation task here, confirming a match between a contribution record and one Wikidata will create a new place_geom record for each geometry the authority record provides. If you prefer to not augment your contribution in this way, un-check the 'Accept geometries...' box before initiating a reconciliation task.

Note that the place_geom records created during Review can always be deleted afterwards from the Reconciliation tab.
